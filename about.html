Our Mission

Our Story

Founded in 2020 by a team of fintech veterans, Stackly started with a simple observation: traditional banking was too slow, too expensive, and too complicated for the digital age.

What began as a simple virtual card solution has evolved into a comprehensive financial platform serving over 1.2 million users across 50 countries. We've processed over $2 billion in transactions and saved our users millions in fees.
