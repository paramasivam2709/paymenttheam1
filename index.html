FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How secure is Stackly?

We use 256-bit SSL encryption, the same standard used by major banks. Our platform is SOC 2 Type II certified and PCI DSS Level 1 compliant. We also offer biometric authentication, two-factor authentication, and real-time transaction monitoring.

Can I use Stackly for business transactions?

Absolutely! We offer specialized business accounts with features like multi-user access, expense management tools, API integrations, and dedicated support. Our Enterprise plan is specifically designed for organizations with advanced needs.

What currencies are supported?

Stackly supports over 50 currencies including USD, EUR, GBP, JPY, CAD, AUD, and major cryptocurrencies like BTC, ETH, and USDC. You can hold, exchange, and transfer between currencies instantly at real exchange rates.

How do virtual cards work?

Virtual cards are digital-only cards generated instantly in our app. Each card has a unique number, expiry date, and CVV. You can create cards for specific merchants, set spending limits, and freeze or delete them anytime. They're perfect for online shopping and subscriptions.

Is there a fee for international transfers?

We charge a flat 0.5% fee on international transfers, significantly lower than traditional banks. Pro and Enterprise users enjoy even lower rates (0.3% and 0.1% respectively). We always use the real mid-market exchange rate with no hidden markups.
